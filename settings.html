Employee Data Management

📋 Required Employee Fields

The system requires comprehensive employee information. All fields marked with * are mandatory for successful import.

📋 Basic Information:
  • Employee ID* (Unique identifier)
  • Full Name*
  • Contact Number* (10 digits)
  • Email Address*
  • Marital Status (Single/Married/Divorced/Widowed)
  • Emergency Contact (10 digits)
  • Address
🆔 Government IDs:
  • PAN Number* (10 characters: 5 letters + 4 digits + 1 letter)
  • Aadhar Number* (12 digits)
  • UAN Number (12 digits - Universal Account Number for PF)
📅 Employment Dates:
  • Date of Joining* (YYYY-MM-DD format)
  • Date of Exit (YYYY-MM-DD format or "Never" for active employees)
💰 Salary Components:
  • Basic Salary* (Base amount)
  • Dearness Allowance (DA)
  • Personal Allowance
  • Conveyance Allowance
  • Professional Allowance
  • Bonus
  • Gross Salary* (Total of all components)
ℹ️ Important Notes:
  • "Never" in Date of Exit means the employee is currently active
  • UAN is required for PF calculations and compliance
  • Government IDs must be valid format for legal compliance
  • Gross Salary should equal Basic + all allowances + bonus
  • System will validate all data before import and show detailed error reports
Supported formats: .xlsx (Excel) and .json files
